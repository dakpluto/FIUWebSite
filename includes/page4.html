2019 Overwatch League Standings


A team's standing is based on both their overall match win/loss record, with ties broken by map differential, head-to-head map record, and head-to-head match record, in that order.

If a tie that affects the qualification for Stage or Postseason playoffs still cannot be broken, the teams will play a tiebreaker match.
